# Cleaner Knowledge Base

> Last updated: January 2026

## Platform Overview

Alicante Cleaners connects professional cleaners with villa owners in the Alicante region of Spain. We focus on quality over quantity - all cleaners are vetted and join by referral only.

## Service Areas

- Alicante City
- San Juan
- Playa de San Juan
- El Campello
- Mutxamel
- San Vicente
- Jijona

## Services & Pricing

| Service | Typical Hours | Description |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Regular Clean | 3 hours | Standard maintenance clean |
| Deep Clean | 5 hours | Thorough clean including hard-to-reach areas |
| Arrival Prep | 4 hours | Pre-arrival preparation with optional extras |

**Hourly rates**: Set by each cleaner (typically €15-20/hour)

## Booking Flow

1. **PENDING** - Owner requests booking, you receive notification
2. **CONFIRMED** - You accept the booking
3. **COMPLETED** - Mark done after finishing, triggers payment

**Important**: Respond to pending bookings within 24 hours to maintain your response rate.

## Teams Feature

### Benefits of Teams
- Coverage for holidays and illness
- Shared client base and referrals
- Team leader can assign bookings to members
- Professional network for support

### Creating a Team
1. Go to Team tab
2. Tap "Create Team"
3. Set team name
4. Share referral link with trusted colleagues

### Team Leader Responsibilities
- Accept bookings on behalf of the team
- Assign jobs to available team members
- Maintain team quality standards

## Profile Optimization

**To get more bookings:**
- Add a professional photo
- Write a compelling bio in Spanish and English
- List all service areas you can cover
- Set competitive hourly rates
- Respond quickly to booking requests
- Encourage clients to leave reviews

## Calendar Sync

You can sync your bookings with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook:
1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "Generate Calendar Link"
3. Copy the link and add to your calendar app
4. Bookings will appear automatically

## Messaging

- You see messages in Spanish (or your preference)

## Payment

- Payment is processed after you mark a booking as COMPLETED
- Payments are made weekly via bank transfer
- You can view earnings in the Home tab

### Common Issues & Solutions

**Can't receive SMS verification code?**
- Check you entered the correct phone number with country code
- Wait 30 seconds and tap "Resend"
- Make sure your phone can receive international SMS
- See full guide: `/join/guide`

**Google Calendar not syncing?**
- Go to Dashboard → Availability → "Connect Google Calendar"
- Make sure you're signed into the correct Google account
- Grant permission when prompted
- We only see busy/free times, not event details (privacy protected)
- See full guide: `/join/calendar-guide`

**Want to edit your profile?**
- Go to Dashboard → Profile tab
- Tap on any field to edit (name, bio, photo, areas, rates)
- Changes save automatically

**Need to update your phone number?**
- Go to Dashboard → Account tab
- Tap "Change Phone Number"
- You'll need to verify the new number with an SMS code

**Want to pause or delete your account?**
- Go to Dashboard → Account tab
- "Pause Account" - temporarily hide your profile (30 days max)
- "Delete Account" - permanent removal after 30-day grace period
